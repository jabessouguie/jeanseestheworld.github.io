# Style Guide — Alpine Spring

## Tone & Voice
- First person, direct, honest
- Concrete numbers over vague superlatives
- Forbidden: "hidden gem", "breathtaking", "must-see", "bucket list"
- Occasionally provocative — challenge conventional travel advice

## Typography
- H1/H2: Playfair Display Bold (700)
- H3/badges/labels: Roboto (600/700)
- Body/lists: Montserrat (400/500)

## Color Usage
- Background primary: #F4F6F2 (snow)
- Background alt (cards, tip-boxes): #E8EDE8 (mist)
- Text primary: #2E4A30 (fern)
- Text secondary: #6B7280 (slate)
- Links/borders/CTAs: #5BA4CF (glacier)
- Price highlights: #E8C547 (alpine_sun)
- Difficulty advanced / alerts only: #C96B3A (wildflower)

## GEO 2026
- Direct answer in the first paragraph after every H2
- FAQ section at the end of every page (5-7 questions for itineraries, 4-5 for city guides)
- Schema.org: Article + FAQPage on every page

## Performance Targets
- LCP ≤ 2.0s
- INP ≤ 200ms
- CLS ≤ 0.1
